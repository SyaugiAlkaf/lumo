HOW IT WORKS

The AI only touches step one.

01

Read

The agent parses the invoice on-device — amount, payee, terms.

02

Decide

A deterministic policy checks payee, limits and whitelist. No model in the loop.

03

Enforce

Soroban releases USDC only if the policy passes. Otherwise, funds stay locked.
